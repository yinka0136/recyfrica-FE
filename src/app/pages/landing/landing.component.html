Why we do what we do?

The main problem lies with single-use plastics, or things we use just one time and then throw away. These include plastic straws, takeout containers, food wrappers, water bottles, and shopping bags. We use and toss away more single-use plastics than we need.

How are we doing this?

  • Stimulating the demand and accessibility to recyclables
  • Raising awareness on the plastic challenge in Africa
  • Fostering collaboration awareness and key partners
  • Advocating for a circular economy
